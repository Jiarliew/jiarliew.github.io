My Role

As an intern, I helped out at multiple departments doing design work ranging from small to large projects. Projects ranged from creating ebooks, posters, marketing material, slideshows, websites, etc.

Designing eBooks

Spent a majority of my internship learning to design eBook covers and laying out content. Learned the value of white space, and the ability it has to provide emphasis. Also created icons and images to help reinforce text.

Designing Posters

There were many times other departments needed design help. The marketing team needed work ranging from creating banners, CTA pages, slideshows, office posters, event pages, and more.

I created posters for the different departments at the company. I had to create a poster that would encourage and also commend the work they do. I took the well known Avengers and designed each department to represent a superhero.

Takeaway

My time as an intern taught me great deal. Certain things just can't be taught in class. Some things I learned include, the pressure of designing a piece that will be seen by the public, or leading a meeting to discuss ideas and projects. I was able to get a look into the life of a designer and the ability we have to impact others with art.
